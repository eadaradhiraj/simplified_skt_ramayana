Narada is the best among the munis.
मुनीषु उत्तमः नारदः अस्ति।
Always undergoing penance.
सदा तपः कुर्वन् अस्ति।
He was eloquent too.
वाक्पटुः अपि सः।
The ascetic Valmiki asked Narada thus -
